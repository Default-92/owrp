BUC error reboot Svalbard

The HPAs on Inuvik antennas are called BUCs, for Svalbard antennas they are called SSPAs.
When an error occurs on the OneWeb Monitoring System - locate the correct LHCP/RHCP BUC or SSPA link from this page or the BUC reboot procedure.
In the SSPA/BUC WebGUI you will have a red "RESET" button in the lower right hand corner of the page if the error persists.
PS: Reset button is only visible when the BUC/SSPA has an error.
