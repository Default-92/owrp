BUC error reboot Svalbard

The HPAs on Inuvik antennas are called BUCs, for Svalbard antennas they are called SSPAs.
When an error occurs on the OneWeb Monitoring System - locate the correct LHCP/RHCP BUC or SSPA link from this page or BUC reboot procedure.
Once you open the GUI you will have a red button in the lower right hand corner of the page, if the error persists click the "Reset error" button.
